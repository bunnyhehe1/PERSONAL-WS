CHINESE OMLET

INGREDIENTS

  • Eggs 2
  • Milk 2 tsp
  • Capsicum 1 /4
  • Onion 1 / 4
  • Tomato 1 / 2
  • Green chilli 1
  • Cabbage 1 leaf
  • Salt 1tsp
  • Chinese salt 1 / 4tsp
  • Black pepper 1 / 4tsp
  • Soya sauce 1 tsp

METHOD

  1. Take eggs and beat them.
  2. Add chinese salt and mix salt, milk, black peper and soya sauce.
  3. Chop vegetables and mix them into eggs.
  4. Take small amount of oil; cook the egg in the fry pain.
  5. After sometimes the omlet is ready.
